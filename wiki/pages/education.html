    Investigation on Agricultural Pests and Carbon Reduction

    Feb 15th–17th, Aug 21st–12th, 2024

    Pest activity has marked seasonal variations and the impact of pests varies from season to season. Research needs to observe and analyze the pattern of pest activities, reproduction and the extent of impact on crops in different seasons. For example, spring may be the peak season for certain pests to reproduce, while summer may be the high season for certain pests to damage crops. Understanding seasonal variations can help develop effective control timing and strategies to respond to pest threats in a timely manner and protect crop growth and development.

    To comprehensively understand pest situation in different regions and seasons. HUBU-China team visited several rural areas in Hubei Province, including Shayang County, Jingmen City, Huangmei County, Huanggang City, Qianjiang City, and Jiangxia District, Wuhan City to investigate agricultural pests and carbon reduction.

    (Upper) The HUBU-China team had an interview with local farmers in February in Huangmei County. (Lower) The HUBU-China interviewed local farmers in August in Jiangxia District.

    Seminar for Scientists from Mozambique

    Prof. Shengchun Li was invited to give a plant synthetic biology topic for biologist from Mozambique at Hubei vocational College of Bio-Technology. Prof. Li introduce recent advances in plastid genetic engineering for crop improvement, especially the plastid-mediated RNA interference (RNAi) for insect management. The different effectiveness of RNAi between Coleoptera and Lepidoptera was paid much attention.

    Mozambique scientist also discussed about the potential and commercial application of transplstomic crops in Africa.

    Synthetic Biology Introduction for High School Students

    Mar 14th, 2024

    Synthetic biology is an emerging cross-discipline, with huge application potential in the fields of medicine, fuels, and environmental protection. It involves the design and construction of novel artificial biological pathways, organisms and devices or the redesign of existing natural biological systems. To introduce the conception of synthetic biology for high school students, HUBU-China team visited and gave presentations in Shashi High School and Yiling High School of Hubei.


    (Left) Prof. Shengchun Li, the supervisor of HUBU-China team, gave a presentation about plant synthetic biology in Shashi High School of Hubei. (Right) HUBU-China team shared pest control via plant-mediated RNAi technology Yiling High School of Hubei.

    Summer Volunteer Teaching

    Jul 1st–7th, 2024

    University education attaches much importance to combine theory and practice, and social practice is a vital approach for college students to understand society and exercise their abilities. As a special form of social practice, volunteer teaching activity is essential for improving the social responsibility and practical ability of college students. In this summer holiday, several HUBU-China members joined in a volunteer teaching group. They went to Chibi city of Hubei Province, and spent one week in helping primary school students study subjects, such as Mathematics and English.

    Besides, PLASTID PESTICIDES™ volunteers also took care of students’ mental health, provided necessary psychological counseling and support, and helped students establish a positive attitude and the courage to face difficulties. Finally, children did some funny experiments with the volunteers’ help, such as simple battery making, egg in a bottle experiment, and boiling water in a paper cup. These experiments increased the attractiveness for children, cultivated their innovative thinking and teamwork abilities.

    In summary, PLASTID PESTICIDES™ volunteer teaching activities bring valuable educational resources to children in rural or remote areas, helping them consolidate their basic knowledge, broaden their horizons, and stimulate their interest in learning.
